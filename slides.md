## Algorithmic Complexity

Understanding performance is crucial for documentation:

**Time Complexity:**
$$O(n \log n)$$

**Space Complexity:**
$$O(n)$$

**Big-O Notation:**
$$T(n) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{1}{i} = O(\log n)$$
